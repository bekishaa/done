# **App Name**: ReceiptRocket

## Core Features:

- Payment Data Input: Capture payment details such as amount paid, payment method, and customer information through a user-friendly form.
- PDF Receipt Generation: Generate well-formatted, professional-looking sales receipts in PDF format from input, using an AI tool.
- Automated Email Dispatch: Enable instant emailing of the generated PDF receipt to the customer's provided email address.
- Internal Receipt Copy: Allow copies of receipts to be sent automatically to internal company teams (e.g., accounting).
- Receipt Tracking Dashboard: Provide a dashboard to track receipt generation and dispatch status.
- Branded Templates: Offer customization options for receipt templates to match company branding.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and professionalism. The sales process has overlaps with finances, so professionalism and trustworthiness are appropriate.
- Background color: Light blue-gray (#F5F6FA) for a clean, unobtrusive backdrop.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) to highlight calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' sans-serif font, to provide a modern, clean, neutral look.
- Use flat, minimalist icons for clarity and a modern aesthetic.
- Ensure a clear, intuitive form layout for easy data entry.
- Subtle transition animations on button clicks and form submissions.
